What is SquareMarket?

SquareMarket is a platform for selling and buying, where one can sell from a single product to multiple products. When a customers wants to buy your product, they contact you and you arrange how to deliver the product to them. Upon delivery, you then get your payment. The following below are the features of our platform

Features

An Easy Way To Sell Your Item

Add a new product with a snap of pictures, then have them shared directly across the SquareMarket platform. With our increasing users, you have a broad customer base and you can arrange how to deliver your product and then receive money upon delivery.

Search For Any Item You Want

This allows you to shop at the comfort of your home. You can search for any product, compare prices and contact the vendor with respect to the product of liking. Then make arrangements on how you can get the product and you pay upon delivery

Easier Management Of Your Inventory

Control everything seamlessly from a single store dashboard where all your inventory will centralized. Right from the palm of your hand, you can manage multiple products and handle delivery arrangements.
